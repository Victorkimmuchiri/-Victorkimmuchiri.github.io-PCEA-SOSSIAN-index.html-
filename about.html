The growth trend

With only two trucks, Noor began in 2010 and has enjoyed steady growth annually. Through the sound and forward-thinking guidance of our dynamic Director, Abdinoor Osman, Noor has prospered through smart expansion, leasing and acquisitions. First moving into East Africa, and then expanding throughout the East and Central Africa, Noor has seized upon opportunity for smart growth. This emphasis on controlled expansion is evident in all undertakings, whether entering new market areas, or increasing service and efficiency through improved equipment and technology.


Noor customers have come to expect safe on- time deliveries of their petroleum products, allowing them to manage their businesses professionally and profitably. Our company’s biggest asset is our flexibility in adapting and communicating with a diverse customer base from order creation to final delivery. Our close partnership with customers allows us to schedule deliveries whenever they are most beneficial, creating true “Just in Time” delivery to maximize capital savings. Our goal is to make sure our customers have the product they need in a timely, unique and safely Noor’s latest venture into the transportation of liquid and dry bulk chemicals continues its course of growth through relevant diversification.





Our job is to ensure that the product we pick up and SAFELY deliver is of the highest quality and is handle. Safety is a core value at Noor and this philosophy is supported throughout the company in everything we do. Management at Noor has established and continually supports the fact that “Safety is Everyone’s Job – All the Time!”
